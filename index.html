Bangladesh 2.0
Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.
Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"
Innovation
Bangladesh's innovation scene is vibrant. From mobile banking to agricultural tech, solutions are emerging to tackle local challenges, driving economic growth and improving lives.
Sustainability
Bangladesh's post-uprising innovations prioritize sustainability. Renewable energy projects, climate-smart agriculture, and waste management solutions are gaining traction, fostering a greener future.
Infrastructure
Bangladesh is rapidly developing its infrastructure. New bridges, roads, and power plants are improving connectivity and boosting economic growth, despite challenges like funding and climate change.
Empowerment
Bangladesh is empowering its citizens through education, skills training, and access to technology. Microfinance and women's entrepreneurship initiatives are fostering economic independence and social mobility.
The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation
Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.
Green Energy
Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.
Modern Infrastructure
Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
